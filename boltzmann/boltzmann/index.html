1. Boltzmann factor

Energy becomes probability.

A physical system can occupy several microstates. Their energies determine relative weights, while temperature controls how strongly energy differences matter.

Boltzmann weights

p(s) = exp[-E(s)/T] / Z,   Z = sum over all states of exp[-E(s)/T]
